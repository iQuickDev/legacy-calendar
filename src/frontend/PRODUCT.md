# Product

## Register

product

## Users
A private friend group. They use this to coordinate events, track participants, and manage shared budgets in a closed, intimate context.

## Product Purpose
To provide a sleek, high-utility calendar that feels like a premium private tool for a close-knit group. Success is frictionless coordination without the noise of public platforms.

## Brand Personality
Sleek, focused, premium.

## Anti-references
- **Light Mode**: Explicitly avoided. No blinding whites or high-key interfaces.
- **Generic SaaS**: Avoid the "dashboard-cream" aesthetic or overly corporate layouts.

## Design Principles
- **OLED First**: Deep blacks and high contrast to favor dark environments and premium displays.
- **Intimate utility**: Information density for power users (friends who know the system) but with a refined, quiet aesthetic.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High contrast for OLED blacks.
- Focus on readability in low-light environments.
